Department of Health Sciences

About Health Sciences Department

Introduction

City University (CUSIT) Peshawar has recently established the Department of Health Sciences. Being a Health Education Department, we are committed to produce high quality Graduates, Medical technologists and Scientists in their respective disciplines, to work, acquire skills and enhance their capabilities to live in rapidly changing global environment. With a dynamic and stimulating educational environment, our tradition is to accept challenges and to receive wisdom today, in order to achieve new things and new approaches for tomorrow. It is currently offering one doctor’s degree (Doctor of Physical Therapy) and one bachelor’s degrees (BS Medical lab Technology). The duration for doctor’s degree duration is 5 years (10 semesters) whereas the Bachelor’s degree is 4 years (8 semesters). The Department is equipped with state of the art equipments in lab and highly qualified faculty.

Our Vision

To be among the best academic healthcare centers in health education, research, and excellence in patient healthcare by using technological advancement, innovative methodology, immense clinical experience, continuous quality improvement, gracious professionalism and life-long learning to achieve the perfection in the health sciences.

Our Mission

Department of Health Sciences, City University is dedicated to educational excellence that embodies the values of caring, integrity, compassion, confidence and acts as an advocate to patient, integrates latest evidence-based practices and with a drive to excel in clinical practices and leadership in the healthcare profession. It strives to establish a centre of excellence in the health sciences and technologies which would play a role of agent for change and a role model for other institutes to emulate. The mission of the Department of Health Sciences (CUSIT) is to produce efficiently trained and specialized professionals who would render their services according to the national and international needs.
